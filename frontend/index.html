How It Works

Three easy steps to a better dining experience

📱
Scan QR Code

Find the QR code placed at the hostel dining hall entrance. Scan it with your phone to open the system instantly — no app download needed.

⭐
Rate Your Meals

Give 1–5 star ratings with comments for Breakfast, Lunch, Snacks and Dinner. The feedback window stays open for 24 hours each day.

🗳️
Vote for Menu

Vote for your favourite dishes each week. The most-voted dish per meal is automatically set as next week's menu — your choice matters!
