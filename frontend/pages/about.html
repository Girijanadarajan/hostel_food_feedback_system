Our Purpose

Why Feedback Matters

Hostel dining directly impacts student health, energy and academic performance. The Hostel Food Feedback System bridges the gap between students and management — making every meal count.

By collecting structured ratings and enabling democratic dish voting, we ensure every plate served reflects what students actually want to eat.

Key Features

What Makes It Work

🔐
College-Only Access

Only @rajalakshmi.edu.in emails are accepted. This ensures all feedback is from genuine hostel students and staff — no outsiders.

⭐
Star Rating System

Students rate each meal from 1 to 5 stars with optional comments. Ratings are collected within a 24-hour window per day.

📱
QR Code Access

A QR code at the dining hall entrance opens the system instantly on any phone. No app download or manual URL needed.

🗳️
Democratic Menu Voting

Students vote for next week's dishes each week. The winning dish per meal is automatically set as the menu — no admin override needed.

⏰
Smart Time Windows

Feedback opens at 5:00 AM and closes 24 hours later. Announcements and voting windows also auto-expire to keep data fresh.

📊
Admin Analytics

Hostel management can view average ratings per meal, most/least liked foods and complete voting results in real-time charts.
